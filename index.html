ASSEMBLY
REQUIRED

ASSEMBLY REQUIRED is an assemblage of 69 works by VCUarts BFA Graphic Design students, Class of 2026. The exhibition joins these projects within a shared structural system. Each unit occupies a distinct position while contributing to a larger collective framework.

Assembled, welded, and bolted into place by Delia Flynn, Eunice Park, Hailey Jo, Kristen Koh, Leya Muwakki, Matt Christian, Megan Shaw, Milena Paul, Ray Burnette, Rusty Johnson, and Sal Scarpa-Friedman.

The build was supervised by Herdimas Anggara.
